Maliza Dozi Compaign summary

The campaigns officially started on 24-08-23 under pharma Africa organization. The star up time frame was only four months from august to December. But we extended the campaigns from recognizing the bigger Gaps. We observed the gap on rational medicine use especially dose accomplishment where statistics shows that 70% of medicine use do not accomplish their doses, which means among three people using medicine one does not accomplish it.

The campaigns have no donor or sponsor but we have been running by our pocket funds. We have done campaigns have involved more than 300 committed youth in delivering to the community. This youth are of different groups such as pharmaceutic profession’s, health professions, other official professions and non-professions. this is all to diving deep into the hearts of youth and infusing the responsibilities’ we have as youth to our country especially in the heath sector and also offering the compass of career journeys that we as youth all have. our studies on why medicine users do not accomplish their doses, also study on what are the effect of not accomplishing /completing the dose.

Methodology on maliza dozi campaigns.

1.Use of digital platforms and social medias.
In this method we created online posters that carry the message of maliza dozi which includes on its cause and negative effects, where also questioner poster which as ask “why don’t you accomplish your dose?” this helped to collect more reasons to why medicine users don’t accomplish their doses. This poster where shared in our social platforms and accounts and on the status of pharma Africa members, every response, question and interaction where recorded and screenshotted for the record. By the online platform we were able to reach 2857 people by the end of December 2023.
2.Use of community outreaches.
On the outreach session we have been able to conduct the campaigns in different populational area and where we knew we targeted most of the victims.
