• Dedicated to the well-being of all people and guided by science, the PAO leads and champions global efforts to give everyone, everywhere an equal chance to live a healthy life • PAO shall always offer tremendous scientific contribution in matters of health importance and drug information in particular through our dedicated commitment. • Founded in 2023, PAO is the non-governmental organization that connects nations, partners, and people to promote health, keep the world safe and serve the vulnerable-so everyone, everywhere can attain the highest level of health. • PAO leads global efforts to expand universal health coverage, and therefore plans for Africa to achieve good health for all using science-based policies and programs • In the country the network officially started operating on 20-may-2023, to adhere to the laws of the country under the NGOs Act No. 24, 2002 as amended in 2005

Our Objectives

  • To fight against drug shortage problem.
  • To conduct health researches and report.
  • foster active cooperation with other International health and drug Agencies, Institutions, Association and Societies of Africa in particular, and the world in general.
  • To promote drug compliance, Rational drug use and provision of Drug information to the society
  • To establish a proper organization basis for communication between the university/college, authorities and students as well as maintaining harmonious relations between Youth within the society.
  • To cooperate and promote relationship between PAO and any other organization which share and promote objectives common to those of PAO.
