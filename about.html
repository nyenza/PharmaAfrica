ABOUT PAO

Founded in 2023, Pharma Africa Organization

(PAO) is a non-governmental organization dedicated to promoting global health and well-being. Guided by science, PAO connects nations, partners, and people to ensure that everyone, everywhere, has an equal opportunity to live a healthy life. Our mission is to lead efforts in expanding universal health coverage across Africa through science-based policies and programs, while offering critical contributions on health and drug information. Officially operating under the laws of the country since May 20, 2023, PAO is committed to serving the vulnerable and keeping the world safe.

Our Mission

To contribute to health & well-being by improving medicines availability and health information through integrated clinical practice, education and research.

Our Vision

We seek for a future with healthy, thriving communities with improved access to drugs, medical supplies and health information to overcome drug and health related problems.
